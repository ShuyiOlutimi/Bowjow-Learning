Welcome To Bowjow Learning

Bowjow learning is an initiative to encourage children to learn using generally available mediums like TVs, Radios and a regular mobile phone. Before COVID-19 UNICEF reports that there were 10,500,000 children out of the school system.

Bowjow aims to create opportunities for inclusion in education. Education for ALL

Vision

Bowjow Learning is an initiative to encourage children to learn using generally available mediums like TVs, radios and a regular mobile phone

Mission

Bowjow aims to create opportunities for inclusive learning

Goal

To be a functional part of learning for 500,000 children between the ages of 2 to 11 in Nigeria by 2021 who would have otherwise had no access to education.

Partnership

Bowjow welcomes partners whose goals align with its own and are willing to support the initiative achieve its goals across various areas. Especially in three main areas:

  • Technology Partnership/Support
  • Content Development Partnership/Support
  • Project Awareness Partnership/Support
